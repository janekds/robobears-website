RoboBears

RoboBears is a competitive combat robotics organization where we design, build and compete combat robots against top Universities. We are dedicated to teaching how to design and manufacture robots from scratch with the intent of competing in our main event each Spring: UC Berkeley's Combat Robotics Competition. We welcome everyone of any major and any experience level. Join our team and experience the thrill of building combat robots!
